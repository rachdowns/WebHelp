Glossary

Actuator

: The portion of a motor-operated valve that converts one type of energy, such as pneumatic pressure, into mechanical energy to operate a valve stem.

Corrosion

: The breaking down or destruction of a material, especially a metal, through chemical reactions. The most common form of corrosion is rusting, which occurs when iron combines with oxygen and water.

Limit switch

: A switch designed to cut off power at or near the limit of travel of a moving object controlled by electrical means.

Torque switch

: A switch designed to sense the amount of torque being applied to a machine by an electric motor. It removes power to the motor if the torque exceeds a preset limit.
