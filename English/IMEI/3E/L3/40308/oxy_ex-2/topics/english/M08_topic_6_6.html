5.6.0 High-Voltage Testing

There will be times when equipment must be tested while power is applied. When these situations occur, the work must be performed only by qualified personnel, and only with special permits signed by top-level managers. If you must work near energized equipment, be sure to obtain all the required special permits and, above all, be extremely careful when installing or removing temporary grounding devices from de-energized circuits located near energized circuits.
