5.0.0 Industrial Craftworker Career Paths

Once you complete your initial training and gain job experience, there are a number of career opportunities available to you as a maintenance craftworker. If you have leadership qualities, you can become foreman of a maintenance crew. Some experienced craftworkers become instructors at contractor, union, or vocational training schools. Or, depending on your level of skill, experience, education, and interest, you may want own your own contracting business. Figure 1 shows general career paths for maintenance craftworkers.
