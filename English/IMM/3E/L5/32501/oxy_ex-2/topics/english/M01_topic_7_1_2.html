6.1.2 Bolt Charts

Most bolt charts provide torque values for bolts up to one inch in diameter. Along with a bolt’s torque value, the charts also may give the number of threads per inch (TPI) or pitch of a bolt, its clamping power, and even its minimum tensile strength. Such factors must be considered before torquing activities are performed. Table 2 shows examples of bolt chart information about three types of 1-inch bolts often found in industry.
