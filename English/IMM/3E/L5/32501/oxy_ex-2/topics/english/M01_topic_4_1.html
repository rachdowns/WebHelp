3.1.0 Solvent Extraction and Dewaxing

Impurities such as inorganic salts and dissolved metals, nitrogen, and sulfur may still remain in some products extracted by the distillation towers. Refining processes such as solvent extraction and dewaxing are used to remove impurities before the products are sent to storage or to another refinery process. Removing the solvent prevents corrosion of the processing equipment. Removing solvent also protects later processes and process equipment.
