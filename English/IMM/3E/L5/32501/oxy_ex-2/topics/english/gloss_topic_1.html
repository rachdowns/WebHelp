Glossary

Calibrated

: Checked or adjusted by comparison against a standard.

Catalysis

: The speeding up of a chemical reaction by a catalyst.

Catalyst

: A substance that speeds up a chemical reaction but is not consumed in the reaction.

Refining

: The process of purifying a substance. Oil refining is an industrial process in which crude oil is refined into petroleum products, such as gasoline, diesel fuel, heating oil, kerosene, and liquefied petroleum gas.

Sour crude oil

: Crude oil containing high levels of the impurity hydrogen sulfide.

Visbreaking

: A refining process that breaks the viscosity of residual oil to increase the yield of middle distillates.
