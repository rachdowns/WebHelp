Overview

Problems with boiler tubes are a significant source of boiler downtime. It is crucial to detect and repair defective tubes as soon as possible. The sooner that tube problems can be detected and professionally re-paired, the less chance defective tubes will cause problems for nearby tubes. By employing a detailed boiler inspection program and applying the proper tools and techniques, forced outages and downtime can be reduced and boiler efficiency can be restored.

Objectives

When you have completed this module, you will be able to do the following:

  1. Identify rolling equipment.

  2. Identify problem tubes.

  3. Explain methods of rolling tubes, plugging tubes, and extracting tubes.

Performance Tasks

Under the supervision of the instructor, the trainee should be able to do the following:

  1. Identify rolling equipment.

  2. Select the tools necessary for rolling tubes.

  3. Identify types of plugs.
