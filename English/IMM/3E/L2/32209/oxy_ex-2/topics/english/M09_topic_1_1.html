Overview

In addition to some of the equipment used with low-pressure steam systems, such as steam traps, boilers, pressure gauges and so on, there are a number of items used in large industrial systems that may run to higher pressure. These include coal-burning equipment that has become more critical to America’s energy future. These involve coal supply systems, coal preparation equipment, and dissolving equipment. Scrubbers are an EPA requirement to clean the exhaust vapors so they meet the Clean Air Act requirements. Fossil-fuel burning requires forced draft air supplies for the boilers. Feedwater systems, including blowdown tanks and evaporators, also become more critical to the industrial process at larger scales.
