6.3.0 Double-Roll Crusher

This design uses two counter-rotating toothed rollers that pull the coal through the crusher. The final size of the coal is determined by the spacing of the two rollers. One of the rollers is spring-loaded to prevent breakage if unbreakable objects such as rocks or debris should enter the crusher. Figure 18 demonstrates the layout of a double-roll crusher.
